FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products and services.

What are food chemicals? +
Food chemicals are substances added to food to maintain or improve safety, freshness, taste, texture, or appearance. They can be natural or synthetic.
Are your products safe for consumption? +
Yes, all our products comply with international food safety standards and are approved for use in food products by relevant regulatory authorities.
Do you offer bulk orders? +
Yes, we offer bulk orders with special pricing. Please contact our sales team for more information.
What is your delivery timeframe? +
Our standard delivery time is 7-10 business days, depending on your location. For urgent requirements, we also offer express delivery options.
